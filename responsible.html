PLAY RESPONSIBLY

The boffins at Prize Snap are committed to providing a safe environment for its players to buy Snap Coins and enjoy the online games. The team at Prize Snap do not encourage excessive playing or the purchase of Snap Coins which extend its players beyond their financial means. If players feel our games are no longer fun, they can delete their account via any of our mobile applications or web page once logged in. We would advise our players seek professional help If they feel this is necessary. 

To help players remain in control, Prize Snap impose a spending limit of £30 maximum per transaction.

Players can see their transaction history from their accounts digital wallet as well as winning history.

TIPS FOR SAFE PLAY

  • Only play with money you can afford to lose – don’t use money which is there to pay rent/mortgages, bills, food, etc.
  • Don’t borrow money to play.
  • Set a spending limit and do not exceed it.
  • Don’t play if you are depressed, upset or under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
  • Balance play with hobbies/activities (work, family, socialising, sports, etc.)
