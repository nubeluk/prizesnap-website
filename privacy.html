Prize Snap are committed to protecting its customers/user’s privacy and will ensure all steps necessary are taken to comply with its legal obligations when using your personal data. Please ensure you read this carefully as this Privacy Policy explains how Prize Snap will fulfil this commitment.

• Technical data - browser version/type, operating system, device information, IP address, network and software identifiers and system configuration

WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR DATA?

We do not pass your personal data onto any third party for the purpose of marketing their products or services. If we decide there is a requirement to pass your data to third parties for the purpose of marketing, we will only do so if we have your consent.

Third parties that help us deliver our products and services to you will sometimes need access to your personal data. These include third parties who assist us with:

• Creating your Prize Snap account (3rd party services such as Apple, Google, Facebook, Twitter etc.)
• Running our competitions (professional judges, auditors and advisors)
• Delivering relevant Prize Snap email marketing (to the extent you have not unsubscribed)
• Investigating fraud or criminal activity
• Other aspects of our service delivery, such as hosting our website and processing customer payments
• Our customer reviews and surveys

If personal data is shared with third parties, we will ensure access is limited to a strictly “need to know” basis and is subject to suitable obligations relating to confidentiality and security.

In addition to the above, we may also be required to share your personal data with third parties if required by regulation or law. In such circumstances, we will ensure disclosure is only to the extent required by law. 

IS YOUR DATA SENT OUTSIDE OF THE EEA?

The EEA (European Economic Area) is deemed to have good standards when it comes to data privacy. As such, we consciously limit the occasions when we may need to transfer or handle your data outside of the EEA. Where our service providers are based outside of the EEA, we make sure that your data is still treated fairly and lawfully in all respects (including making sure we have a legal ground for sending your data outside the EEA and putting in place all necessary safeguards for such arrangement).

HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR DATA SAFE?

We employ industry standard security processes to ensure your data is kept safe and secure, to prevent loss of your data or unauthorised access. We use digital certificated (SSL) and secure server software which encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. We also ensure third parties who need to handle your data when helping us deliver our services are also subject to suitable confidentiality and security standards.
Although we take every measure to ensure your data is sent/received securely we cannot make any guarantee that the information transmitted to us via the Internet will be completely secure and any transmission sent is at your own risk. 

HOW LONG DO WE RETAIN YOUR DATA?

We will retain your data for as long as you hold an account with us and/or where you are still happy to hear from us about our latest news, products and services. Once you no longer wish to take part in our competitions we may still need to keep hold of your data if there is a legal reason for doing so (such as for tax purposes or where we need to resolve any disputes with you).

When we amend our Privacy Policy, it will be published on the prizesnap.co.uk website. If we make any substantial changes, we may also email you if we feel it is appropriate.

YOUR RIGHTS

You are entitled to:

• access a copy of your personal data.
• correct or update your personal data, which you can do yourself by logging into your account.
• restrict and erase your personal data.
• object to the processing of your personal data where we are relying on a legitimate interest (as set out in the above table).

If you want to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details at the bottom of this page. A fee of £25 to cover administration will be charged to exercise your rights. We may need you to confirm your identity before we proceed with your request if it is not clear who is making the request. Where your request is legitimate, we will always respond within one month (unless there is a legal reason to take longer, such as where your request is particularly complex).

You may get in touch with the ICO (Information Commissioner’s Office) if you are concerned about the way in which we are handling your personal data. However, we would appreciate you speaking with us first if you have any concerns.
